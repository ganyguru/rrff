What are we

The Rolling Reels International Short Film Festival (RRISFF) is a wholly student run organization established in the year 2015. It is presented by Festember, the national-level inter college cultural festival of NIT-Trichy, with over a hundred students working on this venture.

What we do

RRISFF is a two day cinematic extravaganza with several events sure to please young film makers and actors. Festember seeks to establish a platform on which such people would get to interact with pioneers in the field that would help hone their skills. The main event comprises of a Short Film Contest which would allow participants to send in their entries for a period of four months (April- July) after which the entries will be filtered by a preliminary panel of judges. The shortlisted movies will then get the opportunity to be screened during the Festival in front of an elite jury comprising of eminent personalities from the film industry.

The winners will not only be the receipt of a plethora of prizes, they would also be able to meet prominent members of the film society and get to interact with them freely. Last year, the jury had two of the biggest directors from the younger generation- Mr. Balaji Mohan and Ms. Madhumitha Sundaraman. The third judge was Mr. Sainath Thotapalli, the famous script writer who has penned over a hundred movies in an array of languages including Tamil, Telugu, etc. The final member of this elite coterie was Dr. Biren Das Shama, a renowned professor of the Satyajit Ray Film Institute, and proved to be an invaluable support to the students of NIT-Trichy as well.

A panel discussion on important film related topics is usually held on the first day with the members of the Jury taking up positions in this panel as well.

A workshop will be held on the second day with experts from a myriad of fields such as direction, cinematography, sound-mixing, etc, coming to impart wisdom to the younger crowd.

Simultaneous screening of Foreign Feature Films will also be done as part of this Festival.
